Railway Facilities

Explore the amenities and services provided by our railways to make your journey comfortable and enjoyable.

On-Board Wi-Fi

Stay connected while you travel. Enjoy free high-speed Wi-Fi available on selected trains to browse, stream, and work on the go.

Food & Beverage Services

Choose from a wide variety of food and drink options to suit your taste. Fresh and hygienic meals are served directly to your seat.

Clean and Comfortable Seating

Enjoy your journey with comfortable seating and well-maintained, clean coaches for a pleasant travel experience.

Accessibility Features

Our trains are equipped with ramps, accessible toilets, and priority seating to ensure a smooth journey for passengers with disabilities.

Power Outlets

Charge your devices during the journey with convenient power outlets available at every seat.
